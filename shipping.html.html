Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Saturday). Orders placed on Sunday or public holidays will be processed the next business day. You will receive a WhatsApp confirmation once your order is dispatched.

Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via WhatsApp. Use it to track your parcel on the courier's website. For any shipping issues, contact us at +92 300 123 4567.

Damaged / Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged, photograph it immediately and contact us within 48 hours. For lost packages, we will investigate with the courier and either resend the item or issue a full refund.
