Orders & Payment

How do I place an order? ▼
Browse our products, click "Add to Cart", then click the Cart button at the top right. Review your items and click "Proceed to Checkout". For payment, contact us on WhatsApp at +44 7508 234409.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), Bank Transfer (HBL, MCB, UBL), JazzCash, and Easypaisa. Payment details are confirmed via WhatsApp after order placement.
Can I cancel my order? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. Contact us immediately on WhatsApp: +44 7508 234409. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
Absolutely. We do not store any payment information. All transactions are handled through trusted payment gateways and confirmed manually for your safety.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 2–4 business days within Pakistan. Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad orders may receive same-day or next-day delivery. Remote areas may take 5–7 days.
Is there free shipping? ▼
Yes! Free shipping on all orders above £50! Orders below £50 have a flat delivery fee of £2.99.
Do you deliver outside Pakistan? ▼
Currently we only ship within Pakistan. We are working on expanding our delivery to international locations soon.
How can I track my order? ▼
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via WhatsApp. You can use it to track your parcel on the courier's website.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 7-day easy return policy. If the product is defective or not as described, contact us within 7 days of delivery. See our full Return Policy for details.
Do products come with warranty? ▼
Yes! Most electronics come with 1-year manufacturer warranty. Warranty details are mentioned in each product description. Accessories have a 3-month warranty.
What if I receive a damaged product? ▼
If your product arrives damaged, take photos immediately and contact us within 48 hours on WhatsApp: +44 7508 234409. We will replace or refund the item at no extra cost.
